About Streetingo

Streetingo is your local guide to delicious, hygienic street food. We use our proprietary algorithm for determining the hygiene of the vendor outlet which includes various factors like safety nets, gloves, pH of water, personal cleanliness etc. Download our app, and discover the best street food in town!
